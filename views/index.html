Awfulix

A movie review app catering to those who enjoy poorly made movies. Awfulix allows users to search movies (supplied by OMDb API), view information about each movie, write reviews on a movie, and rate each movie. I've modified the rating tool to have trashcans in place of stars to add to the theme and promote user friendliness.

Plantastic

An event planning app. User can create, edit, and delete events, attend events and select their contribution which will be displayed along with their name and picture at the bottom of the event page, and comment on events. Users can also see events they are attending on their profile page.

Loxa Script Email

This email that is triggered by a stylist sending their client a recommendation via the CosmoProf app, is composed of HTML, CSS, and AMPScript, the AMPScript is pulling data from the email's data extension to display details about the stylist and client related to the recipient's subscriber key. Loops and if-cases are put in place for proper formatting, rendering comma-separated values (should there be more than one product that exists in the data extension) and preventing errors from occurring in case of missing values, etc.

Click here to read more about Loxa Scripts.

About Me

Hi! My name is Jacquelyn (but you can call me Jackie). I was born in the Central Valley of California, and spent most of my adult life in San Francisco/Oakland. I love web development because it covers my three biggest interests: technology, art, and solving puzzles. I was fortunate enough to study web development in General Assembly's immersive 12 week program, where I learned how to make aesthetically pleasing, fully functional, and responsive websites and apps. I formerly played the role of salon director and art curator at Tailored Salon in the Financial District of San Francisco. I am new to Indianapolis, and I am excited and optimistic in what this new city and I have to offer one another.
