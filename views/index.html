Projects

Awfulix

A movie review app catering to those who enjoy poorly made movies. Awfulix allows users to search movies (supplied by OMDb API), view information about each movie, wrtie reviews on a movie, and rate each movie. I've modified the rating tool to have trashcans in place of stars to add to the theme and promote user friendliness.

Plantastic

An event planning app. User can create, edit, and delete events, attend events and select their contribution which will be displayed along with their name and picture at the bottom of the event page, and comment on events. Users can also see events they are attending on their profile page.

Click here to read about technologies used in this game.

A Picture Worth 10k Character

A creative writing app. Users can get onto the site and write a short story based on the picture prompt. All story details are rendered in a list at the bottom to see the actual story, users can click on the title and the content will display in a modal window.

Broad City Racing Game

A two-player Broad City themed keypress racing game. the characters in the strips are propelled when a player presses either the "a" key or the "l" key. A winner is declared when a player reaches the finish line.

About Me

Hi! My name is Jacquelyn (but you can call me Jackie). I was born in the Central Valley of California, and spent most of my adult life in San Francisco/Oakland. I love web development because it covers my two biggest interests: technology and art. I was fortunate enough to study web development in General Assembly's immersive 12 week program, where I learned how to make aesthetically pleasing, fully functional, and responsive websites and apps. I formerly played the role of salon director and art curator at Tailored Salon in the Financial District of San Francisco. I am new to Indianapolis, and I am excited and optimistic in what this new city and I have to offer one another.
